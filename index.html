Privacy Policy

DailyGroove — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

DailyGroove is a personal fitness and workout timer app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. DailyGroove has no user accounts, no registration process, and no connection to any external server or cloud service.

Data Stored on Your Device

All information you enter into the app (workouts, exercises, session history, and preferences) is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export a backup file.

  • Workout plans and exercise templates
  • Completed session history
  • App preferences (e.g. voice / audio settings)

Backup & Export

The app provides an optional backup feature that exports your data to a file you choose to save on your device. This file is created only when you initiate an export, is saved to a location you control, and is never transmitted anywhere by the app.

Internet Access

DailyGroove does not require or use an internet connection. No data is transmitted to us or to any third party at any time.

Text-to-Speech

If you enable the voice readout feature, the app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to announce workout steps. This is processed entirely on your device; no audio or text is sent to external services.

Third-Party Services

DailyGroove does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions.
